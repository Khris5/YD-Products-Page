    To play,Subscribers will Select five (5) numbers from the possible 69 numbers and Select one(1) number from the possible 26 numbers,Wait for the draw to know the winning numbers for both drums to win, you must match the winning number from left to right. Customer will win if the picked numbers, match the winning numbers revealed during the draw once a week (Saturday 7pm)
