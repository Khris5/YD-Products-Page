  1. Introduction

    2. 1.1 Lagos Family Reality Show is centered on inspiring the spirit of unity and noble values amongst Lagosians, designed as a statewide competition amongst families representing each state of the federation living in Lagos. A Family reality show created to reignite unity and publicity, which elicits the state’s sense of pride and grows knowledge about the country, states, culture, history, and values. The winning family will emerge after surpassing all other families in various competitions that will revolve around advancing our national and state history, values, & culture. The eventual winning Family will emerge as the Celebrity family of the year and will be opportune to have dinner with the Governor. Prizes will be won; also, funds will be made available to the winning family to carry out 10 community projects within the LGA of residence.
