  • Introduction

    • 1.1 Bank3 is a lottery game where one chooses three(3) numers from a possible nine(9) numbers. The drum contains 9 numbers where subscribers are expected to pick numbers from. Three (3) numbers will be drawn from the drum by the subscribers or the subscribers allow the computer to generate the three(3) numbers for the them by selecting quick pick(QP), available when ever a subscriber doesn’t pick his/her numbers for the draw.

    1.2 To play, users just need to subscribe by sending SMS keyword to a short code , dial a USSD or click a web link . Subscription/on demand is charged at a daily fee of N50 and N100 ,N200 for 7 days and N400 for 14 days. User can choose to play multiple times in a day (on demand) to stand better chances.Subscribers will be able to play the on-demand up to a maximum of 10 times in a day, after which they are not allowed to play again until the next day.
