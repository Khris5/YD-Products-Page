  1. Introduction

    2. 1.1 Family Feud is an American television game show where two families compete to name the most popular responses to survey questions to win cash and prizes.

    CONTESTANT RECRUITMENT

    To stand a chance to audition your family for Family Feud Nigeria and win cash on National TV, interested persons are required to send an SMS with FF to 205 or reply to a USSD Prompt Or call 205 and choose the contestant option and follow to the end. SMS/IVR/USSD cost N100 per entry/call.

    HOME QUIZ

    Each week, the home quiz question is asked by the host and displayed visually and viewers are told to send the answer they think is correct. Send SMS as FFH Answer at N50. The answer options are tagged A, B, C, D or E. 20 lucky winners win N20,000.00 each.

    SMS/ MOBILE GAME

    To play, customers can subscribe by sending to the Family Feud DFF to 205 for (5) five questions at a cost of N50, or WFF to 205 for a subscription to get (10) ten questions valid for 1 day at N100. To get additional questions at any time subscribers can send ADD to 205 at a cost of N50 to get five (5) additional questions. At the end of every day, the top 10 subscribers on the leaderboard win N20, 000 each, and at the end of the week, the highest subscriber wins N50, 000 and a pass to the family audition for the live TV show. The weekly winner is invited for auditions and if successful is introduced to his/her family as a weekly SMS Game winner.
