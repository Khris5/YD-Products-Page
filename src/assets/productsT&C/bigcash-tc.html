  1. Introduction

    2. 1.1 Big cash is a game of numbers. To play, users just need to subscribe by sending SMS keyword to a short code or dial a USSD string. Subscription is charged at a daily fee of N50 or N20 per game, where each price point has corresponding prizes. User then selects 5 numbers from 1 to 9 and sends to the short code. User can choose to play more than once in a day to stand better chances. User will be able to play the on-demand up to a maximum of 10 times in a day, after which they are not allowed to play again until the next day.

    Winnings are based on the numbers that matched the winning numbers; the more numbers that matched the wiining numbers, the greater the reward. Example, If user selects 3,2,1,7,2 and winning number is 3,2,8,9,0 ; it means the subscriber has matched 2 winning number from left to right.
